This is Anabelle's Penne Vodka Recipe!

Description

Delicious penne vodka recipe, serves around 4 people

Ingredients

  • 1 lb penne
  • 1 small can tomatop paste
  • as much garlic as you want, i like at least a few bulbs
  • ~1 cup heavy cream
  • a splash of vodka
  • seasonings as desired, i like salt, pepper, red pepper flakes , oregano, and basil
  • a bit of olive oil

Instructions

  1. mince garlic and sautee in olive oil at low heat until golden and fragrant
  2. add salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes
  3. add tomato paste, some basil, and oregano and increase heat to medium, stirring occasionally
  4. Once tomato paste is less bright red, add vodka and continue stirring until dark brick red
  5. Lower heat and slowly add cream until it's the color of an orange
  6. Add pasta and some leftover pasta water and stir to combine
  7. Serve with fresh basil on top
