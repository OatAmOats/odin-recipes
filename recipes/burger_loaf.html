These are my mom's burgers!

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pack ground beef
  • about half an onion
  • garlic, as much as you want
  • breadcrumbs
  • 1 or 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 egg
  • oregano
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. mince onion and garlic
  2. combine all ingredients except olive oil
  3. form into balls, then squish to make patties
  4. cook on a bit of olive oil in pan
  5. serve with rice or in buns with other toppings
