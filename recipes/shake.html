This is my protein shake recipe!

Description

Yummy protein shake, idk the macros or anything but it tastes good and fills me up

Ingredients

  • 2 cups preferred milk
  • 3/4 cup oats
  • 1 banana
  • 1 or 2 scoops protein powder (I like chocolate)
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in blender
  2. Turn blender on
  3. Wait
  4. Turn blender off
  5. Serve
